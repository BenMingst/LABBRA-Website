How LABBRA Works

Our system operates in four critical stages to ensure rapid response and maximum effectiveness.

1

Detection

AI-powered cameras continuously monitor intersection areas, analyzing movement patterns and identifying potential accidents in real-time.

2

Analysis

Machine learning algorithms process visual data to confirm accident occurrence and assess severity level within seconds.

3

Alert

Automatic emergency dispatch with precise GPS coordinates, accident details, and severity assessment sent to 911 services.

4

Response

Emergency services receive comprehensive incident data, enabling faster, more informed response and potentially saving lives.
