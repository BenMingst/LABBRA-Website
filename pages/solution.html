Our Development Plan

This is our vision for how LABBRA will work. We are currently in the development phase and don't have a working product yet. Our goal is to create a system that can contact 911 within 15 seconds of an accident at an intersection.

How We Plan LABBRA to Work

1

Detection

AI-powered cameras will continuously monitor intersection areas, analyzing movement patterns and identifying potential accidents in real-time.

2

Analysis

Machine learning algorithms will process visual data to confirm accident occurrence and assess severity level within seconds.

3

Alert

Automatic emergency dispatch with precise GPS coordinates, accident details, and severity assessment sent to 911 services.

4

Response

Emergency services will receive comprehensive incident data, enabling faster, more informed response and potentially saving lives.
