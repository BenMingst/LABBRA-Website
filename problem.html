Understanding the Scale

The data reveals a growing crisis that demands immediate action and innovative solutions.

Florida Crash Dashboard

Florida Highway Safety and Motor Vehicles (FLHSMV) reports show 686 pedestrian fatalities in 2024, with 10,480 pedestrian crashes reported statewide. This represents a concerning trend that continues to rise year over year.

UCF Area Hotspots

Data from Smart Growth America shows concentrated pedestrian deaths around the UCF campus area between 2008 and 2022. Each incident represents a life lost and a community impacted by inadequate safety measures.
